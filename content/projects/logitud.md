# Logitud — Online store for local authorities

## Overview

As a freelance fullstack developer at [Logitud](https://www.logitud.fr), an established software publisher for municipalities and local authorities, I contributed to an online store platform designed for public-sector organisations. The first production deployment focused on [Mulhouse pools](https://e-services.mulhouse-alsace.fr/__aqua/subscriptions): a generic e-commerce foundation with rules and adaptations specific to that territory.

The system lets agents manage resources with confidence through a **draft and publication workflow** — content can be prepared safely before going live.

## Role & responsibilities

- **Backend (NestJS)**: Bootstrapped the core platform, including the draft/publication organisation
- **Frontend (Nuxt 4)**: Took over the agent interface with Nuxt UI — intuitive workflows for pool staff
- **Pool course planning**: FullCalendar integration and recurring slot logic with rrule
- **Training**: Delivered a 4-day Nuxt training for 30 developers (see [related article](/articles/training-nuxt-interactive-platform))

## Project focus

- **Generic platform, local deployment**: Reusable online store for collectivities, with territory-specific business rules
- **Mulhouse pools**: First concrete rollout — subscriptions and services for the city's aquatic facilities
- **Draft / publication**: Safe content lifecycle so agents can prepare and review before publishing
- **Course schedule**: Planning pool courses and managing recurring slots

## Key achievements

- Designed and implemented the draft/publication foundation on the NestJS backend
- Built an agent-facing Nuxt 4 interface centred on pool course planning and slot management
- Delivered territory-specific adaptations for Mulhouse on top of the shared platform
- Ran hands-on Nuxt training backed by a dedicated interactive platform

## Technical highlights

- **NestJS & TypeORM**: API design, persistence layer, and publication workflow
- **Nuxt 4 & Nuxt UI**: Modern agent back-office with a consistent design system
- **FullCalendar & rrule**: Pool course scheduling and recurring slot patterns
- **Vitest**: Test coverage for critical backend and frontend paths
